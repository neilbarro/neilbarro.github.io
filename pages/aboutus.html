Welcome to BCNSHS: Nurturing Excellence, Inspiring Minds

A Brief History

Baguio City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is a prestigious stand-alone science school located in Baguio City, Philippines. Established in 2017, BCNSHS opened its doors to academically gifted students who successfully passed the rigorous entrance examination. Initially, the school commenced operations with just five sections in the seventh grade, offering specialized strands such as Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM); Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM); General Academic Strand (GAS); Information and Communication Technology (ICT); and Automotive Servicing. With its commitment to providing a top-notch education, BCNSHS has continuously evolved and adapted to meet the changing needs of its students. When the COVID-19 pandemic emerged in 2019, BCNSHS quickly responded by extending admission to transfer students, ensuring that talented individuals from diverse backgrounds had the opportunity to benefit from the school's exceptional programs and resources. In the year 2020, BCNSHS witnessed significant growth and embarked on a new chapter of its journey. This period marked a transformative phase for the school as it expanded its capacity, enhanced its facilities, and broadened its curriculum offerings. BCNSHS embraced innovation and leveraged technology to deliver engaging and interactive remote learning experiences, fostering a dynamic educational environment even in challenging times. The year 2022 proved to be a momentous milestone for BCNSHS when it resumed in-person classes. With the implementation of strict safety protocols and a strong focus on student well-being, BCNSHS ensured a conducive and secure learning environment for its students. This year heralded a renewed sense of camaraderie, collaboration, and growth within the BCNSHS community. Driven by a vision to nurture the next generation of leaders, innovators, and problem solvers, BCNSHS remains dedicated to equipping its students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving world. Through its exemplary faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a comprehensive range of academic and extracurricular opportunities, BCNSHS strives to empower students to realize their full potential and make a positive impact on society. As BCNSHS continues to make substantial advancements, it embraces change, embraces challenges, and embraces the future. With its unwavering commitment to excellence and its firm belief in the transformative power of education, BCNSHS continues to inspire and shape the minds of tomorrow's leaders, fostering a community of lifelong learners dedicated to driving positive change in the world.
