GET TO KNOW BCNSHS

...

Baguio City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is a prestigious stand-alone science school located in Baguio City, Philippines. Established in 2017, BCNSHS opened its doors to academically gifted students who successfully passed the rigorous entrance examination. Initially, the school commenced operations with just five sections in the seventh grade, offering specialized strands such as Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM); Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM); General Academic Strand (GAS); Information and Communication Technology (ICT); and Automotive Servicing.
